AVAPS Reference

Average Volume-Assured Pressure Support · NIV mode for hypoventilation

What is AVAPS?

BiPAP S/T mode with intelligent volume targeting. Automatically adjusts inspiratory pressure support breath-by-breath to maintain a consistent target tidal volume or minute ventilation.

How It Works

Monitors ventilation and increases/decreases PS to hit preset target VT. Includes backup rate for minimum MV. Some devices use auto-EPAP to manage upper airway obstruction without sleep lab titration.

When to Use

Preferred over fixed BiPAP when: concern for failure of standard BiPAP, need for volume targeting, or auto-EPAP would reduce setup burden (OHS, COPD + hypercapnia, neuromuscular disease).

SNF Discharge Criteria: Patient must be medically stable · Clear recovery plan · Not requiring active dose titration or close physician monitoring · Provide 3-day supply of scheduled medications
